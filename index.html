About Me

Starting in programming with Lego RCX robots in 2002 through R.O.S., react, and Android programming in 2021, I have always felt a draw to programming and engineering. I have worked as a technician, tester, researcher, project lead, high school teacher and now I'm also a blogger. This website will have access to my ongoing public projects, some details about my professional history as well as blog posts and tutorials on various topics.

Ongoing Projects

Currently I am working on building up this website, consulting on the Wanda series of UVC robots, refining my Javascript games and Android programming projects. All of these projects tend to revolve around programming in a variety of languages as well as being capable of doing rapid prototyping with 3d printers, cnc mills, and general Mechatronic tasks.
