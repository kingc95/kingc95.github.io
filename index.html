    About Our Team

    Our Mission: To provide a fun, safe, professional and exciting team-based environment, to teach students all of the skills they need to enter any industry and have a grasp on the core concepts of STEM when the graduate.

    Student Lead Team

    The biggest goal every year is to have Students lead and run the team. We want our students to be able to explain to any professional engineer what they did, why, what parts were used and sounds like pros. Our students know the robot inside and out because they build it from start to finish. While we have advisors and mentors we make sure the students do the work, while the mentors teach and guide.

    Our History

    We are a High School Robotics team out of the L'anse Creuse School District. Our team is made up of 9th - 12th Grade students and is Coached and Mentored by industry professionals and former L'anse Creuse alumni. The team began back in 2000. Since then we have continued to compete almost every year in Michigan as well as nationally. Click the button below for more info

    L'Anse Creuse Schools

    Our team is made up of students from the entire L'Anse Creuse Public Schools district in Macomb County. We also allow sending schools that have agreements with our Tech Center to join as well. Starting this year we also have a Middle School after school robotics program. For more info contact us using the info at the bottom of this page.
